🌅 Varanasi – The Eternal City of India

"Older than history, older than tradition, older even than legend…" – Mark Twain on Varanasi

Varanasi, situated in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India, is one of the oldest living cities in the world. It's a major spiritual, religious, and cultural hub of India, located on the banks of the holy River Ganga.

  • Other names: Kashi, Benares
  • River: Ganges (Ganga)
  • Founded by: Lord Shiva (as per mythology)
  • Languages spoken: Hindi, Bhojpuri, English

🕉️ Spiritual and Religious Significance

  • Hinduism: One of the holiest cities. Dying here is believed to free the soul from the cycle of rebirth (moksha).
  • Buddhism: Nearby Sarnath is where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon.
  • Jainism: Birthplace of the 23rd Tirthankara, Parshvanatha.

🧭 Top Tourist Attractions

  1. Kashi Vishwanath Temple
    • Dedicated to Lord Shiva.
    • One of the 12 Jyotirlingas (most sacred Shiva temples).


  2. Dashashwamedh Ghat
    • Most famous ghat.
    • Hosts the grand Ganga Aarti every evening.


  3. Manikarnika Ghat
    • Major cremation ghat.
    • Symbol of life and death cycle.


  4. Assi Ghat
    • Popular among students, travelers, and yoga practitioners.


  5. Sarnath
    • 10 km from Varanasi.
    • Place where Buddha gave his first sermon.
    • Houses Dhamek Stupa and Sarnath Museum.


  6. Banaras Hindu University (BHU)
    • One of the largest and oldest universities in India.
    • Campus includes the New Vishwanath Temple.


  7. Ramnagar Fort
    • 18th-century fort across the Ganga.
    • Contains a museum and vintage artifacts.


🚣‍♂️ Unique Experiences

  • Boat ride at sunrise across the Ganga.
  • Witnessing the evening Ganga Aarti with chants and lamps.
  • Walking in narrow galis (lanes) filled with temples and street food.
  • Exploring old bookstores, music shops, and handloom markets.

🚉 Travel Information

  • By Air: Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport (Varanasi Airport)
  • By Train: Varanasi Junction is well connected.
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March (pleasant weather and festivals)

🎭 Culture

Varanasi is known for classical music, silk weaving (Banarasi sarees), and rich spiritual traditions.
