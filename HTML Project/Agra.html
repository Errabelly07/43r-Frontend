🕌 Agra – The City of Love and Legacy

“Every stone in Agra whispers the stories of love, power, and timeless beauty.”

Agra, located in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, is one of the most iconic tourist destinations in India. Known as the City of Love, Agra was once the capital of the Mughal Empire and is famous for its architectural marvels, historical sites, and romantic charm.

  1. Taj Mahal
    • Symbol of eternal love, built by Emperor Shah Jahan for his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
    • Made entirely of white marble, with intricate inlay work and symmetry.
    • Best time to visit: Sunrise or Full Moon night
    • UNESCO World Heritage Site


  2. Agra Fort
    • A massive red sandstone fortress built by Emperor Akbar.
    • Served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors.
    • Includes beautiful palaces like Jahangir Palace, Khas Mahal, and Diwan-i-Khas.


  3. Mehtab Bagh
    • A charbagh garden complex opposite the Taj Mahal across the Yamuna River.
    • Best for sunset views of the Taj Mahal's reflection.


  4. Fatehpur Sikri (nearby)
    • A perfectly preserved ghost city built by Akbar, 40 km from Agra.
    • Major attractions: Buland Darwaza, Jama Masjid, Panch Mahal, Tomb of Salim Chishti.
    • UNESCO World Heritage Site


  5. Itimad-ud-Daulah's Tomb (Baby Taj)
    • Often called the “jewel box,” it is a prototype of the Taj Mahal.
    • First Mughal tomb made of white marble.


  6. 🛍️ Shopping in Agra
    • Sadar Bazaar – Leather goods, handicrafts, sweets.
    • Kinari Bazaar – Jewelry, marble artifacts, zardozi embroidery.
    • Shahganj Market – Clothes, accessories, home décor.

  7. 🍛 Local Food to Try
    • Petha – Sweet made from ash gourd (famous specialty).
    • Mughlai cuisine – Rich gravies, kebabs, and biryanis.
    • Bedai and Jalebi – A popular breakfast combo.
    • Dalmoth – Spicy dry snack.


  8. ✈️ How to Reach Agra
    • By Air: Nearest airport – Agra Airport (Kheria), or Indira Gandhi International Airport (Delhi).
    • By Train: Agra Cantt, Agra Fort, and Raja Ki Mandi stations.
    • By Road: Connected via Yamuna Expressway (from Delhi – ~3 hrs drive).
  9. 🏨 Where to Stay
    • Luxury: The Oberoi Amarvilas (with Taj view), ITC Mughal
    • Mid-range: Hotel Taj Resorts, Crystal Sarovar
    • Budget: Zostel Agra, goSTOPS Agra
  10. 📅 Best Time to Visit
    • October to March – Pleasant weather for sightseeing.
    • Avoid summers (April–June) as it can get extremely hot.
